## Creating Your First Link

Shortening a Spotify URL with sptfy.in is quick and easy. Follow these steps to create your first link.

### Basic Link Creation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get your Spotify URL">
    Copy any Spotify link from the Spotify app or website. You can shorten:
    - **Tracks** - Individual songs
    - **Albums** - Full albums
    - **Artists** - Artist profiles
    - **Playlists** - Your playlists or public playlists
    - **Podcasts/Episodes** - Shows and episodes
    - **User profiles** - Spotify user pages
  </Step>

  <Step title="Paste the URL">
    On the [sptfy.in homepage](https://sptfy.in), paste your Spotify URL in the input field.
    
    **Paste Options:**
    - **Manual paste** - Right-click → Paste or Ctrl+V (Cmd+V on Mac)
    - **Paste button** - Click the clipboard icon next to the input field
    - **Auto-extraction** - The system automatically extracts valid Spotify URLs from any text you paste
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete verification">
    For security, you'll see a Cloudflare Turnstile verification widget. In most cases, this validates automatically without any action required.
    
    <Note>
    The verification helps prevent abuse and spam while keeping the service free for everyone.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click 'Short It!'">
    Once verification is complete, click the **Short It!** button to create your shortened link.
    
    The button will show:
    - **"validating..."** - While verifying with Turnstile
    - **"loading..."** - While creating your link
    - **Shield icon** - Verification status (green check = ready)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Copy your link">
    Your new short link appears instantly! Use the action buttons to:
    - **Copy** - Click the copy icon to copy the link to your clipboard
    - **Open** - Click the external link icon to test your shortened URL
    - **Show QR** - Generate a QR code for easy mobile sharing
    - **Show Clicks** - View analytics for your link (see [Analytics guide](/guides/analytics))
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Spotify Link Expansion

sptfy.in automatically expands `spotify.link` short URLs to their full `open.spotify.com` format before shortening.

<Tip>
If you paste a `spotify.link` URL, you'll see a toast notification saying "Link expanded!" as the system resolves it to the full Spotify URL.
</Tip>

**Why this happens:**
- Ensures your sptfy.in link points to the permanent Spotify URL
- Prevents double-shortening issues
- Provides better analytics and reliability

## URL Validation

sptfy.in only accepts valid Spotify URLs from these domains:
- `open.spotify.com/*` - Standard Spotify links
- `spotify.link/*` - Official Spotify short links (auto-expanded)

### What Happens If URL Is Invalid

If you paste a non-Spotify URL, you'll see an error dialog:

**Error: No Spotify URL Found**
> The clipboard content doesn't contain a valid Spotify URL. Please copy a link from Spotify and try again.

<Warning>
sptfy.in is designed exclusively for Spotify URLs. Other link shorteners exist for general-purpose URL shortening.
</Warning>

## Generated vs Custom Slugs

By default, sptfy.in generates a random 4-character slug for your link:

- **Example:** `sptfy.in/a7x9`
- **Format:** Lowercase letters (a-z) and numbers (0-9)
- **Length:** Starts at 4 characters, increases automatically if needed

Want a custom slug instead? See the [Custom Slugs guide](/guides/custom-slugs).

## Rate Limits

To keep the service free and prevent abuse, sptfy.in has rate limits:

<Warning>
**Rate Limit:** If you create too many links too quickly, you'll see:

**Error: Too Many Requests**
> You've created too many links recently. Please wait a moment and try again.

Wait a few minutes before creating more links.
</Warning>

## Link Preview

When you paste a Spotify URL, sptfy.in fetches a rich preview showing:

- **Album/track artwork** - Visual thumbnail
- **Title** - Song name, artist name, or playlist title
- **Subtitle** - Artist, album info, or description
- **Type badge** - Content type (track, album, artist, playlist, etc.)
- **Green checkmark** - Indicates valid Spotify content

**Loading states:**
- Skeleton animation while fetching preview
- Preview appears within 1-2 seconds
- Falls back to music icon if preview fails

## Privacy & Terms

By creating a link, you agree to sptfy.in's [Privacy Policy](https://sptfy.in/about/privacy) and [Terms of Service](https://sptfy.in/about/terms).

Key points:
- Links are **public** and can be accessed by anyone
- Analytics data is **anonymized** (see [Analytics guide](/guides/analytics))
- sptfy.in **does not collect** personal information from link creators (unless you sign in)
